What is an impossible list?

You might be wondering, what is this, like a... list which contains your goals which you feel are impossible but is actually possible? Well... yeah, that is pretty much it.

Conquering your goals is something I believe in. Don't you have a bucket list? Haven't you ever put your heart and soul into something and when you finally achieved it, nothing, not even Thanos could bring you down? Yeah? This is my bucket list.

P.S. This was inspired from an impossible list that Thomas Frank has.

Why have you put it online for the world to read it?

I realise that this is a very personal section of my life, but I believe that this will not only fast-track me to achieve my goals but actually enable me to achieve them.

Have you heard of this theory? It basically tells you to not reveal your goals to anyone until some concrete progress is made. Else, you will feel a pseudo-acheievement feeling, which prevents you from actually starting to act on the goal.

Then why are you revealing it to the world? By revealing my impossible list to you, I'm holding myself accountable. I am put under pressure to achieve these goals. If I don't act on these goals, I know that I will either forget it, or simply regret it (which is worse than failure).
